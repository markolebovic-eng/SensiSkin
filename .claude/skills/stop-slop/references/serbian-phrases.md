# Serbian AI Tells — Banned Phrases and Patterns

## Throat-clearing openers (Serbian)
- "Važno je napomenuti da..."
- "Treba istaći da..."
- "Kao što smo već videli..."
- "U zaključku možemo reći..."
- "Naime,"
- "Pre svega,"
- "Generalno gledano,"

## Emphasis crutches (Serbian)
- "svakako"
- "bez sumnje"
- "zaista"
- "zapravo" (when used as filler, not for genuine contrast)
- "naravno"
- "očigledno"

## Passive constructions to avoid
- "može se reći"
- "treba napomenuti"
- "mora se uzeti u obzir"
- "smatra se"
- "poznato je da"

## Vague declaratives (Serbian)
- "Ovo je veoma važno."
- "To je ključno."
- "Rezultati su impresivni."
- "Pristup je sveobuhvatan."

## Business jargon (Serbian)
- "u kontekstu"
- "iz perspektive"
- "na nivou"
- "u okviru"
- "implementirati" (when "primeniti" works)
- "optimizovati" (when "poboljšati" works)

## Connector overuse
- "takođe" (more than once per 300 words)
- "kao i" (as filler connector)
- "pored toga"
- "štaviše" (when not genuinely additive)

## Structural AI tells (Serbian-specific)
- Lists of three where two would suffice
- Starting consecutive sentences with the same word
- Ending post with generic encouragement ("Nadam se da vam je ovo pomoglo")
- Defining a term in the first sentence of a post
