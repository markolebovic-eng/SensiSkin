The product-marketing.md tone description ("kao razgovor sa pouzdanim doktorom koji vam je prijatelj") describes the aspiration, not the reality of Nataša's actual blog posts.

Fetched posts (8 analyzed, 2026-07-02) show:
- Formal to neutral addressing — "Vi" in CTAs, "mi" for studio perspective, not warm "ti"
- No personal anecdotes from private life
- Opening patterns: grouping reader ("Za sve vas koji..."), normalizing ("NORMALNA POJAVA"), or scientific statement
- CTA always: direct, transactional ("Pozovite nas i zakažite besplatne konsultacije [phone]")
- Technical parameters used as authority signal (633nm, 415nm, 6-8 tretmana 2x nedeljno)
- No emojis, no exclamation points in body text
- ALL CAPS used occasionally for 2-3 key concept words per post

**Why:** The product-marketing.md was written from positioning aspirations, not from corpus analysis. The brand-voice-script.md (created 2026-07-02) is the accurate reference.

**How to apply:** For SensiSkin blog content, use the "Ton i glas" section in product-marketing.md (added 2026-07-02) and brand-voice-script.md as primary voice reference. The "warm doctor friend" framing applies better to service pages and CTAs, not blog body copy.
