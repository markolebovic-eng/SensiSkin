As of July 2026, these topics have confirmed Serbian-language editorial demand and thin salon/practitioner blog coverage — prime SEO opportunity window:

1. **Skin longevity** — Covered by lepotaizdravlje.rs (April 2026), wannabemagazine.com (Jan 2026), ninanatural-cosmetics.rs (April 2026). Zero Serbian salon/clinic blogs addressing it from practitioner angle. Term used as English-Serbian hybrid ("skin longevity tretmani") — this is how users actually search.

2. **LED terapija svetlom / LED fototerapija** — At-home LED mask boom driving educational queries. Multiple .rs product/portal articles exist but zero salon-practitioner educational blogs in Novi Sad. Distinct from service-page content (which only covers "what we offer").

3. **Analiza kože pre tretmana / dijagnostika** — Personalization wave of 2026 is generating "how do I know what my skin needs" queries. Serbian SERP near-empty for educational practitioner content. Croatian clinic pages (VISIA, Bagatin) dominate — opportunity for Serbian salon to rank.

4. **Eksosomi/egzosomi** — Growing but more medically positioned; not yet mainstream enough for a cosmetic studio blog post in Serbia. Better fit for medical aesthetic clinics. Watch for late 2026.

**Why:** Research conducted July 2026 using live WebSearch across .rs publications, competitor salon blogs, and regional beauty press.
