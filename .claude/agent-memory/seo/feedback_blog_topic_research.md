When doing blog topic research for a Serbian beauty/aesthetic clinic, this sequence reliably produces validated, gap-based recommendations:

1. Search for "[topic] blog srpski 2026 kozmetički centar site:rs" to check what Serbian salon blogs have actually published — not just what portals cover
2. Search for leading .rs beauty portals (lepotaizdravlje.rs, wannabemagazine.com, harpersbazaar.rs) to identify editorially confirmed trends — these lead salon blogs by 1–2 months
3. Search competitor salons directly (Vita Elos, Divine Beauty Novi Sad, Studio LiliuM, K2 Derma) to confirm what they have NOT written — the gap is the opportunity
4. Cross-check against international trend signals (Croatian/regional beauty press is ~1–3 months ahead of Serbian salon content)
5. Map every candidate topic to a specific service the client offers — informational blogs must support a service without cannibalizing its keyword

**Why:** The most valuable blog topics are those where search demand exists (confirmed by editorial coverage) but quality Serbian-language practitioner content does not — the SERP gap, not just the trend.

**How to apply:** Always run both the "what exists" and "what's missing" searches before recommending a topic. Never recommend a topic just because it is trending internationally — confirm it has reached the Serbian editorial layer before calling it actionable demand.
