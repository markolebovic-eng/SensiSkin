2. TYPO: "pisajte u komentarima" → "pišite u komentarima". Fixed.
3. CTA FORMAT: Instagram draft buried the phone number mid-sentence with lowercase "pozovite". Locked rule requires the CTA to END with the exact phrase "Pozovite nas na 065/333-8-338" as a standalone closer. Restructured Instagram closer. Fixed.

LESSON FOR FUTURE SOCIAL/COPY TASKS: Always run a Latin-script + CTA-format check on agent output before delivery. Cyrillic characters can slip in invisibly when an agent types Serbian. The CTA must be the final sentence in exact format, not embedded.
